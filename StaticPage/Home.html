Introduction

My dream is to provide a quality education to our children. The children are highly educated but they do not have sense of responsibilities, moral values and know the power of education. So even after education our childrens are behind in all sense. The education they get are not used anywhere in life. They sheer waste their precious time of their life in schools.


My aim is to provide a sense of value. In our traditional way of schooling; in our madrasas and gurukul the children are taught moral educations, which forms the basis of our society. Physical education to build immunity against germs and stay strong in life. Love for nature, understand the gift of God; our beautiful nature and take care of it. Teach the fundamentals skills that are necessary to earn living.
